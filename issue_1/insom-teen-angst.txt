Dark
~~~~

Under a dim light, dark and scary,
In a room, small, dank and dreary,
Through a hall, and up the stairs,
He huddles up in the corner there,
Little boy lost, fear encroaching,
Hears a tap something approaching,
Sobbing and crying, scramble away,
Four walls no doors, have to stay.
